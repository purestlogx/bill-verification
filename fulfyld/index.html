FF_STANDARD rate card

Edit any rate below, then Save. Weight brackets and zone columns are fixed labels — you’re editing the dollar amounts. Invalid or blank cells are blocked on save. Use Restore built-in card to undo edits. (If Fulfyld changes the weight brackets themselves, tell me and I’ll ship an updated build.)

General

Fuel is checked as a band, not just a ceiling. This bill runs 8% (USPS) and 11% (other carrier); set the floor and cap to your contracted terms. Lines above the cap are flagged Over, below the floor Under (a possible mis-rate). Set floor to 0 to disable the lower bound.
