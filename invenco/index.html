Invenco Fulfillment Bill Verificationv1.10.2

Verifies the Invenco billing detail (AUD) — line arithmetic, fulfillment fees against your contracted (+4.2%) rate card, packaging, duplicate charges, grand-total reconciliation, and domestic Standard & Express shipping against the zone × weight card plus the fuel-surcharge band. Shipping weight is estimated from SKU weights, so it is approximate — in-band lines pass with their exact surcharge %, lines below the band are flagged Under card and above it Over card. Storage is excluded by choice.

Verification rules

Rules apply on the next run and are saved in this browser. The fulfillment rate card holds your contracted rates (defaults are the +4.2% column). A fee type is matched by Sub Category + Charge Type, exactly as they appear in the bill.

Fee types not listed here (e.g. shipping, storage) aren’t rate-checked — they’ll appear under “Fulfillment lines without a contract rate” so you can add them if needed.

Packaging price card (base, AUD)

Packaging TypePrice each (AUD)

A packaging charge is valid if its rate matches any price here. Rates that match nothing are flagged.

SKU weight rules

Same rules as the MY Order Processing Tool — one source of truth. They drive both picking units (via pack expansion) and shipping weight (via de-bundling). A SKU must start with prefix and, if a suffix is set, end with it (first match wins; blank suffix matches any ending). A SKU matching no rule has no weight, so its shipping line shows “cannot verify” — never assumed.

Bundle SKU overrides

Auto-decode already expands the standard bundles (e.g. PN-BUNDLE-CGB630+SOSE30 → its two component packs; PNSG-CGB1xBCCE1 → two each-packs). Add a manual override only for a bundle the auto-decoder can’t read — it then expands into the exact components you list. Overrides take priority over auto-decode.

Shipping fuel-surcharge band

Billed domestic shipping should equal the card rate plus a fuel surcharge in this range. Lines inside the band pass (their exact % is recorded in the Shipping rate-check sheet); lines outside are flagged for review.
